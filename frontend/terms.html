2. About AECAS

The Association of Engineering Construction and Architecture Students (AECAS) is a student organization at the Technical University of Kenya (TUK). Our mission is to unite students in Engineering and the Built Environment, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration that bridges academic learning with practical industry experience.
