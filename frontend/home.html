About AECAS

We are a vibrant community of future professionals at the Technical University of Kenya.

Motto

Fostering interdisciplinary collaboration in engineering and the built environment

Mission

To unite students in Engineering and the Built Environment, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration that bridges academic learning with practical industry experience.

Vision

To be the leading student association connecting academia, industry, and communities for a resilient future in engineering and construction.

What We Do

We organize regular visits to construction sites, engineering projects, and industrial facilities. Students get exposed to real projects and observe modern building techniques, engineering processes, and industry best practices.
